About the Creator

Creator Game

A creative sandbox game where you can build worlds, customize characters, and bring your imagination to life!

Features

  • Unlimited workspace with pan and scroll navigation
  • Build Mode: Place shapes and create structures
  • Play Mode: Interact with your creations
  • Custom Shape Creator: Draw your own unique shapes
  • Character Customizer: Design animated characters
  • Save & Share: Export and share your worlds
  • Dark/Light Mode themes

Controls

  • Build Mode: Click shapes, then click canvas to place
  • Navigate: WASD or Arrow Keys to pan
  • Delete: Right-click or Delete Mode
  • Drag: Hold Shift + Click to drag blocks
  • Play Mode: WASD or Arrow Keys to move character
